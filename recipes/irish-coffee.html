Irish Coffee

Morning Guinness?

Ingredients

Makes 1 Drink
  • 1½ ounces Irish whiskey
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Heavy cream, whipped slightly
  • Fresh brewed coffee, hot

Steps

  1. Pre-heat a stemmed mug with hot water, then empty it.
  2. Add whiskey and sugar to glass, and fill with hot coffee to half-way. Stir thouroughly until sugar is dissoved.
  3. Fill the rest of the cup with hot coffee, stopping about an inch below the rim, and giving the contents one final stir.
  4. Top with slightly whipped cream by pouring it over the back of a spoon.
