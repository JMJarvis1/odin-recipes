Corpse Reviver No. 2

A Pre-Prohibition Classic!

Ingredients

Makes 1 Drink
  • 1 ounce gin
  • 1 ounce Cointreau, Grand Marnier, or other orange liqueur
  • 1 ounce Lilliet Blanc, or sweet white vermouth if unavailable
  • 1 ounce freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 3 drops of Pernod, absinthe, or similar

Steps

  1. Combine with ice in a cocktail shaker and shake until thorughly chilled
  2. Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with a cherry
