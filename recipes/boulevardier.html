Boulevardier

Paris and New York Collide in this Prohibition Era Cocktail

Ingredients

Makes 15-20
  • 1-½ ounces bourbon
  • 1 ounce campari
  • 1 ounce sweet vermouth

Steps

  1. Combine with ice in a cocktail shaker or separate glass and stir thoroughly until chilled
  2. Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with a cherry
