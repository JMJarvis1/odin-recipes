Blue Moon

A Mid-Centuries Night's Dream

Ingredients

Makes 1 Drink
  • 2 ounces gin
  • ½ ounce crème de violette
  • ½ ounce freshly squeezed lemon juice

Steps

  1. Combine with ice in a cocktail shaker and shake until thorughly chilled
  2. Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with a strip of lemon peel
